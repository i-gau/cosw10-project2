What is it?

A cured meat dish originating from France around the 15th century, it became popular in the USA around the 1990s.
The dish also includes other finger foods than just meat, see next ingredients section.

Ingredients List

  • a mixture of soft and firm cheeses
  • cured meats
  • bread
  • olives
  • pickled foods
  • dried and fresh fruits
  • condiments:
    • honey
    • marmalades
    • mustards
    • preserves
    • jams

Preparing the board

Just place the ingredients however you like. There is no wrong way to do it. It's going to taste great no matter what the arrangement looks like.

  1. Get a tray, preferably something wooden.
  2. Arrange ingredients on tray. Get creative!
  3. Place utensils for the condiments.
  4. Pour some wine.
  5. Feel fancy eating your bougie lunchables with some good company.
